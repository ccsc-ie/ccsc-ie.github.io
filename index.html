Mission statement

The CCSC is a friendly community based sports complex in Charleville. Our aim is to provide fun, enjoyable environment that encourages all participants to reach their full potential no matter what their aspirations may be. We will do this by providing the best possible facilities in an environment that aims to foster children's and adults' wellbeing. Our success will be measured by the pride our community members take in the sports complex and the role models we produce within the community.

Purpose

Grow, manage and promote all available sports within the complex. Provide a fun and safe environment. Promote the welfare and the children and the adults. Produce role models within the community. Be an active participant in the Charleville community and beyond.

Values

Provide a fun, safe and friendly environment for children to learn life skills. Display and expect respect and tolerance towards each other. Encourage all to have a go and contribute to the sports complex.
